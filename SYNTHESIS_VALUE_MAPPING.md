PROFESSOR-CONVINCING PITCH:

Rather than forcing a linear read through 8,000+ words, we chunk the synthesis into

"This section translates the literature into a repeatable process. A researcher doesn't need

5. DECISION FRAMEWORK (Lines 88-105)
================================================================================
THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES:
"I have multiple options from the literature. Which is RIGHT for MY situation?" 
Researchers need context-aware decision support, not universal recommendations.

HOW IT PROVIDES VALUE:
• Weighted evaluation matrix (Performance 30%, Effort 25%, Scalability 20%, Resources 15%, Maintenance 10%)
• Customizable scoring: "Rate: 1-10" for their specific constraints
• Literature-based weight justification
• Guidance for each criterion (not just numbers)

PERSONA WORKFLOW:
Researcher rates their situation: "My enterprise has limited budget (Resources: 3/10), 
experienced team (Effort: 8/10)." Weighted score guides them toward OSPF over RIP.

PROFESSOR-CONVINCING PITCH:
"The Decision Framework is where the synthesis becomes personalized. We provide the 
weights based on literature analysis, but researchers apply them to their unique context. 
It's not 'Here's the best answer' - it's 'Here's how to choose based on YOUR constraints.' 
This respects researcher autonomy while providing decision support. It's actually solving 
the persona's core problem: moving from 'What SHOULD I do?' to 'What's RIGHT for me?'"


6. SUCCESS METRICS FRAMEWORK (Lines 108-129)
================================================================================
THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES:

measurable targets extracted from literature.

HOW IT PROVIDES VALUE:
• Primary metrics with specific targets (e.g., "< 5 seconds convergence time")
• Measurement instructions (HOW to collect the metric)
• Validation references (which papers support this benchmark)
• Secondary metrics for comprehensive evaluation

PERSONA VALUE:
Researcher implements OSPF, then checks: "Convergence time < 5 seconds? ✓ Bandwidth 
efficient? ✓ Scalable? ✓" Clear pass/fail criteria from literature.

PROFESSOR-CONVINCING PITCH:
"Success Metrics close the feedback loop. Researchers don't just implement - they validate 
against literature benchmarks. This creates accountability and evidence-based iteration. If 
convergence time exceeds 5 seconds, researchers know to investigate why (implementation issue 
vs. different assumptions vs. network constraints). It's not just doing research; it's doing 
it with measurable rigor."
